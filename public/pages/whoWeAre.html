Who we are


Our History


For over 50 years we have been working in Milan and hinterland to improve the health and quality of life of people with disabilities, promoting their well-being, autonomy and social inclusion, through services provided by health and social areas, for over 1500 users.
We address people with disabilities, mainly in the developmental age (from 0 to 18 years) affected by psychomotor, psycho-affective and neurodevelopmental disorders, without forgetting the adult band. For us any rehabilitative intervention does not only have a medical-sanitary character but must enhance the capacity of the individual in its integrity, sensitizing at the same time the external environment in a perspective of inclusion. We have obtained the quality certification according to the UNI EN ISO 9001: 2008 Regulations for all the services offered in the Socio-Sanitary, Training, Personal Projects and Guidelines, to guarantee and protect the people and bodies that choose us every day. ONLYUS of Milan Onlus operates on four accredited sites (Milan, Monza, Pavia, Como) contracted with the reference ATS (Milan, Monza, Como) and with some District Districts and municipalities in the area.


Our Values


  1. ANALYZE THE NEED

    Each activity is based on an in-depth analysis of the children's life context and the social fabric of belonging, through the integrated relationship of different professional skills.

  2. OPERATE IN A PREVENTION OPT

    Our activities take into account the close correlation between the precocity of the intervention and the improvement of the quality of life of the child and his family.

  3. INVESTING ON PROFESSIONALITY

    The specific training and long experience of our clinical teams guarantee highly qualified interventions in the treatment of children with psychophysical discomfort.

  4. OFFER INNOVATIVE INTERVENTIONS

    The continuous comparison with the most up-to-date research and international clinical experiences guarantee the innovativeness of our services.

  5. CONSIDERING THE PERSON IN ITS GLOBALITY
    To meet the needs of every child our Center is designed to accommodate and respond to all accessibility needs.

    In order to allow the care of families from all over Italy and abroad, our Center is equipped with a small residence available free to accommodate all those who make / request.

    To guarantee the right of reading to all children with motor and communication disabilities, the first Special Library was created to open the world of books and fantasy to them too.


Our Mission


The care of the child is at the Onlyus. Our mission is to help children with psychological problems and serious communication disabilities through the work of two clinical sectors:

  • Psychotherapy
  • Alternative Augmentative Communication

We offer highly specialized services aimed at the child and the family starting from the analysis of the life context and the social fabric of belonging, with constant attention to the clinical quality, analyzing the need, investing on professionalism to offer innovative interventions. In this way we operate with a view to prevention and personal care, focusing on the precocity of the intervention and on improving the quality of life of the child and his family.
